# 📊 Accessories Sales Analysis

**v1.0** · Comprehensive Sales Performance · Sep 2025 – Jan 2027

---

## 📋 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive sales performance analysis of accessories products across multiple regions. The dataset includes **500 transaction records** from **September 2025** through **January 2027**, providing deep insights into revenue patterns, product performance, regional dynamics, and customer behavior.

Product categories include: **Backpack, Chair, Charger, Desk Lamp, Notebook, Pen Set,** and **USB Drive**. Sales are analyzed across four regions: **East, North, South,** and **West**.

## 🔍 Key Business Insights

### 💰 Revenue Concentration
- **Top 3 Regions:** South (32.2%), West (30.4%), East (20.2%)
- **Top 3 Products:** Backpack, Pen Set, USB Drive (48% of total revenue)
- **South + West:** Combined 62.6% of total revenue

### 📦 Product Strategy Recommendations
- **Backpack:** Premium positioning with highest revenue potential
- **Pen Set:** Volume-driven growth opportunity (424 units)
- **USB Drive & Charger:** High-frequency items for cross-selling
- **Desk Lamp & Chair:** Underperforming — consider promotions

### 📍 Regional Strategy
- **South:** Maintain leadership with premium product focus
- **West:** Close gap through marketing campaigns
- **North:** Untapped potential — increase product awareness
- **East:** Steady growth opportunity

### 📅 Seasonal Patterns
- **Peak Season:** July – October (45% of annual revenue)
- **Low Season:** February & June (6% of annual revenue)
- **Recommendation:** Run targeted promotions during low months
