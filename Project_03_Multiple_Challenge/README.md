# 📊 Excel Data Analytics & Business Intelligence Challenges

Apnar Excel data analysis ebong business intelligence projects er jonno ekta comprehensive repository. Ey project e bivinno real-world datasets (FMCG Sales & HR Employee Attrition) niye conditional logic, formulas, pivot tables, ebong dynamic dashboards er kaj kora hoyeche.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

Ey repository te total **5ti major data challenges** solve kora hoyeche, jekhane advanced data manipulation techniques use kora hoyeche. 

### 📁 Datasets Used
1. **Dataset 1 (FMCG Sales Data):** Territory, Category, Brand, Order Qty, Delivery Qty, ebong Delivery Amount (TP) er details distribution analytics.
2. **Dataset 2 (HR Analytics / Employee Attrition):** Employees' demographics, job satisfaction, education level, work-life balance, ebong monthly salary trends tracking.

---

## 🔍 Detailed Breakdown of Challenges

### 🔹 Challenge 1: Conditional Logic & Performance Metrics
* **Task 1:** Target vs Sales calculation (`IF` logic use kore comparison). `Sales > Target` hole `Achieved` ebong clear status filtering.
* **Task 2:** Dynamic Commission model preparation. Sales Target cross korle **10% commission** are na korle **0% commission** automate kora.

### 🔹 Challenge 2: Advanced Data Counting & Validation
* **Task 1 & 2:** `COUNTA` ebong specialized count formulas dynamic strings ebong numerical input tracking er jonno.
* **Task 3:** Blank fields identification (`COUNTBLANK` algorithm).
* **Task 4:** Specific criteria check (Question 4 baseline context calculation).

### 🔹 Challenge 3: Conditional Aggregations (`SUMIF` & `SUMIFS`)
* **Task 1:** Month-wise specific distribution filter validation (`Month = 12` er unique revenue extract kora).
* **Task 2:** Company-specific metrics calculation (e.g., "Tesla" total sales evaluation pattern).

### 🔹 Challenge 4: FMCG Sales Pivot Distribution Report
* **Territory Analytics:** Top performance area track kora via Dynamic Pivot View.
* **Category & Brand Matrix:** Delivery amount (TP) breakdown for categories like `MILK`, `BISCUIT`, `CRACKERS`, `NOODLES`, etc.
* **Special Filter:** Only `Diploma Brand` performance context check.

### 🔹 Challenge 5: HR Analytics Dashboard & Trend Identification
* **Demographics:** Gender distribution & Education background breakdown.
* **Employee Sentiment:** Job satisfaction rate vs Work-life balance impact assessment.
* **Financial Trends:** Monthly salary spreads over multiple years of experience for Full-time & Contractor roles.
* **Comprehensive Dashboard:** Pivot charts dynamic interconnectivity implementation.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Key Formula Frameworks

* **Platform:** Microsoft Excel / Google Sheets
* **Advanced Formulas:** `=IF()`, `=COUNTIF()`, `=COUNTBLANK()`, `=SUMIF()`, `=SUMIFS()`
* **BI Feature:** Pivot Tables, Interactive Slicers, Timeline, Conditional Formatting Data Bars.
