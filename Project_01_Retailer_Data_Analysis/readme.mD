<b>Beverage Retail Sales Analysis Project</b><br>
<b>Project Overview</b></br>
This project presents a comprehensive sales performance analysis of beverage products across four major retail chains: Costco, Target, Walgreens, and Walmart. The analysis encompasses sales data from January 2022 through December 2022, providing insights into revenue patterns, product performance, regional market dynamics, and operational metrics.

1. Sales Performance & Volume
Total Revenue by Retailer: Assessment of revenue contribution across retail partners

Top Performing Retailer: Identification of highest revenue-generating channel

Best-Selling Beverage Brands: Analysis of unit sales volume by product

Average Price per Unit: Comparative pricing analysis across brands

2. Temporal Trends
Monthly Sales Growth: Tracking sales volume progression throughout 2022

Seasonal Patterns: Identification of peak sales periods and demand fluctuations

3. Geographic & Regional Insights
Regional Market Share: Analysis of sales distribution across Northeast, South, and West regions

Brand Popularity by State: Product preference patterns by geographic location

4. Operational & Data Integrity
Data Source Audit: Verification of data completeness (288 total records)

Contact/Account Coverage: Assessment of account coverage consistency

Price Variation Analysis: Examination of price range and variance by brand

5. Retailer Comparison
Retailer × Beverage Brand Matrix: Cross-tabulation of sales volume by retailer and product

6. Advanced Business Insights
Revenue Concentration: Analysis of revenue distribution across product categories
